Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

At Digitme.fun, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website digitme.fun and use our applications, including the Reverse app.

The short version: we don't collect your personal data.

1 Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Our website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics tools, or any other form of data collection. We do not store your IP address, browser information, or any identifiable data.

2 Our Applications

Reverse App

The Reverse app is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No data collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.
  • No account required: You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.
  • Audio stays on your device: All voice recordings are processed and stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers.
  • No advertising: The app does not contain any ads or advertising SDKs.
  • No analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking tools within the app.

3 Microphone Permission

The Reverse app requires access to your device's microphone to record audio. This permission is used solely for the core functionality of the app — recording sounds that you choose to record. Audio data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted over the internet
  • Never shared with third parties

4 Third-Party Services

Our website and applications do not integrate any third-party services that collect personal data. We do not use:

  • • Google Analytics or any analytics platform
  • • Advertising networks (AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
  • • Social media tracking pixels
  • • Third-party login services

5 Children's Privacy

Our applications are suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data whatsoever, our apps are safe for children to use. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

6 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
