friendping! — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

At Digitme.fun, we take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use friendping!, our mobile application.

In summary: we do not collect any personal data.

1 Data Collected

We do not collect any personal data.

friendping! does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics tools or any other form of data collection. We do not store your name, phone number, contacts list, IP address, device information or any other identifiable data.

2 friendping! App

friendping! was designed with your privacy in mind:

  • No data collection: we do not collect, store or transmit any of your personal data.
  • No account required: you do not need to create an account or provide personal information to use the app.
  • Contacts stay on your device: your contacts list is accessed locally and only to suggest a friend. No contact data is ever sent to our servers.
  • No advertising: the app contains no ads or advertising SDKs.
  • No analytics tools: we do not use any analytics or tracking tools within the app.

3 Contacts Permission

friendping! requires access to your device's contacts to randomly select a friend. This permission is used only for the app's core functionality — picking a random contact to encourage you to reconnect. Your contacts data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted over the Internet
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never stored outside your device

4 Third-Party Services

friendping! does not integrate any third-party services that collect personal data. We do not use:

  • • Google Analytics or any analytics platform
  • • Advertising networks (AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
  • • Social media tracking pixels
  • • Third-party login services
  • • Crash reporting tools that collect personal data

5 Children's Privacy

friendping! is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data, the app can be used safely by children. We comply with applicable regulations relating to the protection of minors' data (GDPR, COPPA).

6 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
